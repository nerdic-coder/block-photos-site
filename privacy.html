Privacy Policy

 

1. Privacy Commitment

Block Photos is committed to protecting the privacy of our users’ personal information.

This statement applies with respect to the information that we collect from the marketing website at https://block-photos.com/ and the application's provided to our customers, and users of the free, open source product located at https://app.block-photos.com/. Amendments to this statement will be posted at this URL and will be effective when posted. Your continued use of this site following the posting of any amendment, modification or change shall constitute your acceptance thereof.

2. Information collected

We do not collect any personally identifiable information about you unless you voluntarily submit such information to us, by, for example, signing up for an e-mail subscription.

3. Use of information

We will only use the information you provide to us for the purpose for which such information was provided.

4. Use of cookies

When you view Block Photos Website or use the Block Photos app we might store some information on your computer. This information will be in the form of a "cookie" or similar file. Cookies are small pieces of information stored on your hard drive, not on our site/app. Cookies do not spy on you or otherwise invade your privacy, and they cannot invade your hard drive nor steal information. Rather, they help you navigate a Website as easily as possible. We use cookies to store that users are logged in and to gather aggregate information via our analytics provider.

5. Security

Block Photos uses industry standard security measures to protect the loss, misuse, disclosure, and alteration of the information under our control. All other data is owned by each individual user and under that user's control. There is no central database that would expose user data, so the Block Photos reminds users that they must secure their individual devices to maintain the highest level security possible.

6. Right to access

Block Photos service allows its users the right to access their data or modify information previously provided. Since each user owns their data, they can delete or modify that data within the Block Photos app or they can delete the data by accessing the storage provider they have selected.
